Tools:

Butter Knife
Spoon
Plate

Ingredients:

2 Slices Bread
1 Jar Peanut Butter
1 Jar Jelly

Steps:

1. Lie slices of bread next to each other on plate.
2. Using butter knife, scoop some peanut butter out of the jar and spread it on one of the slices of bread.
3. Using spoon, scoop some jelly out of the jar and spread it on one of the slices of bread.
4. Take the slice of bread with peanut butter on top, flip it, and place on the slice with jelly.
5. Enjoy!
